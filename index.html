Tap Clash Duo

Tap your side to build charge. Reach 100 to fire a blast!

How to Play

  1. Each player taps their half of the screen to charge up.
  2. When your charge hits 100, you auto-fire a blast.
  3. Blasts remove one heart. First to land 3 blasts wins!
  4. Watch for ⚡ Boost buttons for bonus charge.
